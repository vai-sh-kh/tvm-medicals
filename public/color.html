Usage Guidelines

  • Navy Blue (#1B3B6F) - Conveys trust, professionalism, and medical expertise. Use for headers, navigation, and trust-building elements.
  • Green (#2BA84A) - Represents health, wellness, and positive outcomes. Use for CTAs, success states, and health indicators.
  • Neutral Grays - Provide clarity and readability. Use for body text, borders, and backgrounds.
  • Semantic Colors - Communicate status clearly. Use consistently for success, warning, error, and info states.
